Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sentinel Check collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Contact details (such as your name and email address)
  • Information you provide when contacting us or booking a check
  • Basic technical data (such as IP address and browser type) via web server logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Respond to enquiries
  • Provide cybersecurity assessments and services
  • Improve our website and services

3. Data Storage & Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational steps to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse, in line with UK GDPR obligations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share information only when required by law or when strictly necessary to deliver our services.

5. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict how we process your data
  • Withdraw consent where applicable
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO (ico.org.uk)

6. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for business or legal purposes, after which it is securely deleted or anonymised.

7. Cookies

This website does not use tracking or advertising cookies. No analytics scripts are loaded. Basic server logs may be retained for security and performance purposes only.
