The Truth About Incognito / Private Mode

WHAT IT DOES
  • Doesn't save your browser history locally
  • Doesn't save cookies after you close the window
  • Doesn't save form data or passwords locally
  • Keeps different sessions isolated from each other
WHAT IT DOES NOT DO
  • Hide your IP address from websites
  • Hide your activity from your ISP
  • Hide your activity from your employer (on work networks)
  • Prevent browser fingerprinting
  • Stop trackers on the page from running
  • Hide what you're doing from Google if you're logged in
Google settled a $5 billion lawsuit in 2024 over collecting data from users in "private" Chrome windows. Incognito does not mean invisible.
